# Simple Roasted Chicken

A fast-cooking & easy chicken with a crispy skin and a juicy inside.

## Ingredients

  • 1 Roasting Chicken
  • Salt, Pepper
  • About 4 feet of twine

## Directions

Preheat the oven to 450˚F

Rinse the chicken and remove any giblets that might be inside. Dry the chicken as thoroughly as you can. The goal is to remove as much water as possible — water will create steam, which will reduce the crispyness of the skin. Let the chicken sit for 5 minutes to sweat, then pat dry again.

Coat the skin with plenty of kosher salt and pepper. If you have some fresh herbs, make small cuts in the skin and stuff the herbs underneath.

Truss the chicken and place into the smallest roasting pan (or skillet) that it will fit in.

Bake for about 20 minutes, then flip the chicken so it faces breast side down. Cook for another 15 minutes, then flip over again. Continue roasting until interior temperature reaches 140˚F - about another 10-20 minutes. Total roasting time is about 40-50 minutes.
